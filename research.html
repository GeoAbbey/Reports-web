Research Projects


We offer a wide variety of high-quality marketing research support services. We value most our relationships with clients and partnering with them to achieve their goals. We have worked with academic institutions, such as the Lagos Business School on a Bill & Melinda Gate Foundation-funded report.
We specialise in market-entry research for clients investing in Nigeria, as well as landscape, benefit and impact assessments. Data Collection through innovative tools and e-platforms using qualitative and quantitative methodology is one of our strengths. We have conducted Knowledge Aptitude and Perception (KAP) surveys in the public health space with significant samples sizes across Nigeria.
We support clients with overall project management of detailed report writing as well as providing creative design support through our partners. With a team of writers, we offer master reviewing and editorial services as well as advise on report content and structure.
